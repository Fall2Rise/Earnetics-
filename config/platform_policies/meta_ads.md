# Meta Ads Platform Policy (Facebook & Instagram)

**Last Updated:** 2024-01-15  
**Platform:** Meta (Facebook, Instagram)  
**Risk Level:** HIGH (AI review + human review, account restrictions common)

## Core Policies

### Prohibited Content
- Discriminatory practices
- Tobacco and related products
- Drugs and drug-related products
- Unsafe supplements
- Weapons, ammunition, explosives
- Adult products and services
- Payday loans, paycheck advances
- Multi-level marketing
- Penny auctions

### Restricted Content
- Alcohol (age/location targeting required)
- Dating services
- Online gambling and gaming
- Prescription pharmaceuticals
- Supplements (requires pre-approval)
- Financial services and products
- Branded content (requires tag)
- Social issues, elections, politics (requires authorization)

### Ad Content Requirements
- **Text Limit:** Avoid excessive text in images (20% rule relaxed but still impacts delivery)
- **Prohibited Claims:** No "before/after" images without disclaimer; no misleading health claims
- **Targeting:** No discriminatory targeting; special ad categories for housing/employment/credit
- **Engagement Bait:** No "like/share/tag" prompts
- **Sensational Content:** No shocking, graphic, or excessively violent imagery

### Landing Page Requirements
- Functional and accurate
- Matches ad content and offer
- No pop-ups or interstitials that obstruct content
- Clear and accessible privacy policy
- Secure (HTTPS recommended)
- Mobile-friendly

### Special Ad Categories
If advertising housing, employment, credit, or social issues:
- Limited targeting options
- "Paid for by" disclosure required
- Additional authorization needed

## Pre-Publish Checklist
- [ ] No prohibited content
- [ ] Restricted content has proper authorization
- [ ] Landing page matches ad promise
- [ ] Privacy policy accessible
- [ ] No engagement bait language
- [ ] Image text <20% (use text overlay tool)
- [ ] No before/after without disclaimer
- [ ] FTC disclosures for affiliate/sponsored content
- [ ] Age-gating for restricted categories
- [ ] Special ad category declared if applicable

## Violation Response
1. **Ad Rejection:** Edit and resubmit
2. **Account Restriction:** Limited ad delivery; fix violations
3. **Account Disabled:** Appeal within 30 days; provide documentation
4. **Permanent Ban:** Multiple violations or severe policy breach

## Meta-Specific Best Practices
- Use "Learn More" CTA for educational content
- Avoid "you" language that feels invasive
- Test multiple creatives (algorithm favors variety)
- Use Advantage+ placements for better delivery
- Monitor relevance score and adjust

## Earnetics-Specific Rules
- All affiliate content must have "Paid partnership" tag
- Income claims require "results not typical" disclaimer
- Health/wellness products need pre-approval
- No retargeting without privacy policy disclosure
- Maintain 2.0+ relevance score or pause
